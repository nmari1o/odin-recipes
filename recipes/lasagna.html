Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a very popular dish amongst almost all cultures in the world, globally. It contains several ingridients which combined make harmonyc taste. There are lasagnas for no-vege people and for vege people.

Ingridients

  • pound sweet Italian sausage.
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef.
  • ½ cup minced onion.
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed.
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes.
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste.
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce.
  • ½ cup water.

Steps

  1. Put pasta water on to boil.
  2. Brown the ground beef.
  3. Cook the bell pepper, onions, garlic, add back the beef.
  4. Transfer to medium sized pot, add tomatoes and remaining sauce ingredients to build the sauce.
  5. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles.
  6. Heat the oven to 375°F.
  7. Assemble the lasagna.
  8. Bake.
